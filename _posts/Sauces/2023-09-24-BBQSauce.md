## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 Onion
- [ ] 400g Chopped Tomatoes
- [ ] 1tsp Garlic Granules 
- [ ] 80g Brown Sugar
- [ ] 3tbsp Malt Vinegar
- [ ] 2tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
- [ ] 1tbsp Tomato Pur√©e

## Method
1. Cook the onion in a pan with some oil.
2. Add all the rest of the ingredients.
3. Blend once cooled a little.
4. Pour into a jar for use.
